Overview

The CR-56 AMAX appears in Call of Duty: Warzone. It was added in June of 2021 as part of the Season 5: In Deep Water update. The CR-56 sports a four-shot kill at close to mid-range with its default ammunition, while M67 Ammo sports a two-shot kill, though it converts the weapon to semi-automatic and decreases magazine capacity. Although recoil is obvious, it can be easily controlled, like the HBRa3. At close-range, the firerate tends to prevent the four shot kill from taking affect, making it slightly unsuitable for CQC, and at long-range, the CR-56 falls flat, like its peers, against Marksman Rifles and Sniper Rifles.
