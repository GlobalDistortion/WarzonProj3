Overview

The Karabiner 98k, abbreviated as Kar98k, is a German bolt-action rifle featured in every game of the Call of Duty series set during World War II, as well as appearing in the Rezurrection map pack or the Prestige or Hardened version of Call of Duty Black Ops in Zombies mode, and in Call of Duty: Mobile and Call of Duty: Modern Warfare. It is used by German soldiers and is usable by the player, but is rarely seen in use by other troops of the Allied forces.
